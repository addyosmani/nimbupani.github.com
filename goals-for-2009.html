Goals for 2009

Here is what I hope to achieve in 2009 (given that I hardly achieved anything I set out to achieve in 2008). I hope to keep blogging my progress with my goals.

Blog/Interest Goals

  • Blog at least once a week
  • Complete reading Web Analytics book
  • Upgrade Blog to Drupal 6 (waiting for all my cherished modules to become available in Drupal 6!)
  • Implement SEO strategies while analysing metrics for my blog (and blog about it!)
  • Read W3C’s HTML 4 specifications
  • Read Sitepoint’s CSS Stylesheet reference (and blog about this and the above)

Personal Goals

  • Go to Gym every alternate day
  • Lose 5 Kgs and keep those kilos off

  • Attend web specific conferences like SXSW/Web Directions North
