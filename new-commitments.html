New Commitments

Dec 1st, 2005 | Comments

I have just recently planned my paintings which resulted in two good ones. Now, reading the Artists Manual is like finding a treasure house and I just cant wait to complete it and start painting with the new found knowledge of art resource management :) So now that the year is coming to an end here are a list of things I am committed to doing:

  • One Graphic Illustration Per Week
  • One painting every two weeks
  • Gym every week
  • Swimming every two weeks
  • Be in communication with friends every two weeks (atleast!)
  • Participate in Word It for December
  • Read the 10+ graphic design e-books and books by Jan 15th 2005
  • Set up automated backups and version control process for my illustrations and designs by next week
  • Review two artists works every month
  • Review a movie every week

That should be enough for now. And you, as my readers, do make sure I honour these words :)
