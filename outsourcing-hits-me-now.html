Outsourcing Hits Me Now!

A few months ago, I was rejected by DBS when I applied for my credit card. I had applied again (assuming it was my bad handwriting, but received no response from them. And today, I got a call now from one of the call centers for DBS in India (specifically South India):

“I am calling from DBS Bank, blah blah..you can enjoy lower/higher interest rates with your DBS Credit Card funds. And..” “I dont have a DBS credit card.”

[A Pause]

“But you have applied for that right?” “Yeah, but I have not received any response yet.”

“Oh okay, you will be getting it soon. Thank You. Good Bye.”

Weird!
